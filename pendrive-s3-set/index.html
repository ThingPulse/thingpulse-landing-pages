Under The Hood

Its open-source firmware encourages exploration and customization, while the integrated WS2812b RGB LED provides real-time visual feedback to monitor device status and script execution.

  • USB-C Interface: Ensures broad compatibility and easy connectivity.

  • WiFi Capabilities: Remote deployment of payloads for comprehensive security evaluations.

Write or import scripts using our open-source firmware. Adjust settings to fit your workflow, whether you’re automating everyday tasks or performing penetration tests.

Have Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

What is the Pendrive S3?

The Pendrive S3 is a versatile USB-C dongle designed to automate tasks and perform penetration testing. It simulates keyboard and mouse events, allowing you to execute scripts that control your computer—automating virtually everything you can do manually with a keyboard and mouse, from launching applications and typing messages to moving the cursor.

What operating systems are compatible with the ESP32-S3 Pendrive?

The ESP32-S3 Pendrive is designed as a USB HID (Human Interface Device), which means it’s recognized as a standard keyboard and mouse by most operating systems. This makes it compatible with a wide range of platforms including Windows, MacOS X and most Linux distributions. Many smart phones are supported as well

Can I use this device for commercial applications?

Yes, the Pendrive S3 is designed for versatility and can be used in commercial settings. Its open-source firmware and USB HID functionality make it an excellent tool for automating tasks and performing penetration tests in a professional environment. However, if you plan to integrate it into commercial workflows, it’s important to:

  • Ensure Compliance: Verify that your usage complies with local regulations and industry standards, especially for security testing.
  • Adhere to Best Practices: Use the device within ethical and legal boundaries, particularly when deploying payloads or automating actions on networks.
  • Consider Customization Needs: Take advantage of the open-source nature of the firmware to tailor the device for your specific business needs, whether for automation, training, or cybersecurity evaluations.
By keeping these points in mind, you can confidently incorporate the Pendrive S3 into your commercial operations.

Which programming skills are required?

The Pendrive S3 uses a simple, easy-to-learn scripting language to simulate mouse and keyboard commands, so you don’t need to be an expert programmer. Even if you’re new to coding, you can quickly grasp the basics and start automating tasks right away. With our examples both beginners and experienced users can customize the device to meet their needs.
